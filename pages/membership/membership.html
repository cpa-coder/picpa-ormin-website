PICPA Glue Up

Registration Instruction

1

Login to https://picpa.eventbank.com/ and click “REGISTER”

2

Select “REGISTER WITH EMAIL”

3

Fill up the necessary information.

For Password – you should input your desired password; there is NO default password setup.

Further, do not forget to opt-in to PICPA and EventBank’s Terms of Use for Users and Privacy Policy as these are mandatory.

4

Once registered, you will be redirected to PICPA Homepage https://picpa.eventbank.com/my/home/

You are now -

  • Registered both as a PICPA CRM Contact and an EventBank registered user and NOT as a PICPA member.
  • Will be asked to complete your EventBank Profile.

    • You may skip this portion, however, the pop-up window will re-appear after each login until filled-up

  • You will be asked to verify your registered e-mail address. To verify, access the verification e-mail sent by Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants no-reply@eventbank.com to registered e-mail address’ Inbox/Spam Folder.

    • Click "VERIFY YOUR EMAIL"

In case, verification e-mail was not received click “RESEND VERIFICATION EMAIL” on the homepage.

In case, verification of e-mail address was skipped or not done after registration, upon your next login, the system will prompt you to verify your registered e-mail address first otherwise you will not be able to proceed.

To verify, click “OK” and access the verification e-mail sent by PICPA EventBank to your registered e-mail address’ Inbox/Spam Folder

In case, verification e-mail was not received click “CLICK HERE” to resend the verification e-mail.

Once the e-mail address is verified, you are redirected to PICPA Homepage URL.

5

Submit/apply/create your respective PICPA membership application to become a PICPA member for the current fiscal year (e.g. July 01, 2021 to June 30, 2022)

On the left side bar either click “Become A Member” under PICPA or the Memberships Menu. Yor are then redirected to PICPA Memberships URL https://picpa.eventbank.com/my/memberships/discover/

Click “APPLY” on the respective membership type you wish to apply for.

Under Membership Selection, you are provided information as to the selected membership type. If satisfied click “APPLY” otherwise select another membership type.

Under Application Details, you are required to fill up the following member application details:

  • Personal Information Details

    Certification Details

    Upload copy of PRC ID for faster approval and/or verification process.

  • Employment/Business Details

    PICPA Membership Details

    If already paid, upload proof of payment for faster approval and/or verification process.

  • Once done, click “SAVE”. An e-mail will be sent by no-reply@eventbank.com to registered e-mail address with subject Membership Application Status - Awaiting Approval
